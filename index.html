ABOUT SIOM

The Symbiosis Institute of Operations Management (SIOM), Nashik, is a one-of-a-kind school that falls under the umbrella of Symbiosis International (Deemed University) and provides world-class education in Operations Management. SIOM provides value-based educational skills that chisel engineers into excellent business techno-professionals atop today's competitive markets, based on the concept of "promoting international understanding through quality education."

SIOM, a specialised institution with a distinct emphasis on operations, has a tailored curriculum aligned with business and has consistently provided consultancy and business advising services to various industries. SIOM provides real-time business solutions through collaborations between academics and industries, making this institution a centre of business excellence to give organisations the analytical know-how. SIOM aims to develop students' mind-sets and skills to manage ambitious company growth by obtaining cost excellence, fast acquiring market excellence, strengthening and adapting the design, and expanding global and regional operational footprints. It instils practical and analytical capabilities in potential leaders with a comprehensive knowledge base.

TATTV 2024

Tattv, the annual Operations Summit is a flagship event organized by SIOM in an attempt to congregate eminent leaders and industry experts exhibiting engaging sessions, addressing pertinent issues of Operations and Supply Chain and sharing insights into the best practices across industries. With cutting-edge technology and accelerating innovation increasingly driving the business world, the focus on Operations as the conjecture of optimising and sustaining business processes has been growing immensely.

As it becomes pivotal for the managers to emulate and keep up with the industry standards and changing dynamics of the global business world, the annual conclave hosted by SIOM provides the forum, facilitating dialogues between visionaries and thought leaders from the Operations world as they discuss and deliberate, enabling networking and benchmarking ideas and practices.

THEME

PRAVAAH : Cascading Through Perpetual Challenges

Earlier it was believed that identifying risks would mitigate the chance of meeting vulnerabilities. We have transformed from an era of speculation to a period of sustainability. The continuous transformative changes across the supply chain network have bought possibilities to mitigate the risk of any uncertainty. Now, the time has come to think about streamlining the flow of these new developments while keeping in mind four major strategic areas to focus on: recovering revenue, rebuilding operations, rethinking the organization and accelerating the adoption of digital solutions. The best part of the uncertainty is the certainty of having it at some point in time. The time has come to postulate a roadmap enabling organizations to sail smoothly through unforeseen turbulence.

Symbiosis Institute of Operations Management introduces PRAVAAH: Cascading through perpetual challenges as the theme of Tattv 2023. We are looking forward to the minds who mitigated the threat of operational disruption and laid initiatives to bring change to their existing processes. At Tattv 2023, we aim to transform through our subthemes that embrace the existence of resilience, Integrity and sustainability. We are excited to nourish the need for digital transformations on the wheels of the economy. We aim to hover the last-mile delivery area within the core operational values: volume, variety, variation and visibility. We seek to change the future and invite you to be part of Tattv 2023.
